Catch Your First Wave — Everyone’s Welcome.

Everyone Surf School is a beginner-friendly surf project made for all wave lovers — no matter your age, background, or experience. We believe surfing is for everyone. Our mission is to create an inclusive space where you can learn, have fun, and feel connected to the ocean. Join us for good vibes, soft boards, and salty smiles.
